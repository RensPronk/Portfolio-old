For a school Project I had to do a redesign of an existing website called “Direct.nl”. It was a local electronics shop based in Rotterdam. their previous website was a mess and I had to come up with a new design using the right design patterns.


The previous design of the website was outdated and not pleasant to look at. They needed a new website that would be more appealing. I designed the new website with design patterns as part of the assignment. As deliverables I designed all the pages they needed to order a product. Including homepage, productpage, payment pages and more. For this assignment I used Figma to create the prototypes.
