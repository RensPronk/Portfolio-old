Tolk to me

Cases

For a school project We had to make an app for an actual client. We had to create a concept that would help deaf people in the city.


We came up with the “Tolk to me” App which allowed deaf people to get in contact with more translators since our research showed there were not nearly enough translators available. After we were done with the research side of the project we started on designing the app. We used Figma to design the app and make the prototypes. Research told us what our target audience needed and we made sure to provide it within the app. We created all the neccessary pages to get a working prototype.


When we finished to prototype we did some user test. Together with one other group member we went to a festival for deaf people and setup a stand for testing. At the end of the day we got some good feedback and testing date.


After the tests we improved our app with the feedback we got and handed in all our research and app design to the client.
