In my first year of school I had a project where I had to re-design a website for a local theater. It's called the Imperium theater.This assignment came from the theater itself and the goal was to re-design a part of the website for a specific target audience.


I had the target audience of renting. I chose 6 pages I needed to redesign and started working.

I had to start at the beginning of the cycle. The first things I did were writing a debriefing so that I had a better view of what I was suppose to make. From there I started working on the first stage.


After the preparation I started working on the prototypes. I made sketches of what I wanted to make. Then followed that up by making Wireframes in Axure and later Using HTML and CSS to build the actual website.


In the end I deliverd a log stating all the things I did during the project including reflexions on my work. I also deliverd a projectfile with all the work I did for the project with explainations. Last but not least I deliverd a functional website that people at Imperium can use.
