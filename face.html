App Design

For a school project we had the assignment to make an app to “get color into your life” For the brand called “FACE Stockholm”. We created a live wallpaper app with customizable backgrounds.  The backgrounds change depending on your surroundings. Part of the assignment was to make use of the sensors on the phone.

The app was made in Figma using only google’s material design.


We started off with researching the target audience. Then we started working on the functionality of the app, We started off with simple wireframes. We decided to go with material design because it looks great and has a very pleasant user experience. The app is relatively simple. You have 3 tabs. Each one is neccessary to get the final wallpaper activated. On the first tab you select which sensors to use.  the second tab is for which theme your want.


And the last tab is for the colours you want along with some additional settings. In the end we handed in a fully functional prototype and our research for the app.
